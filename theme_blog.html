Enchanted Kingdom

The magic that stays with You

When In Manila, way back October 1995. Enchanted Kingdom opened its doors to the public. I still remember the excitement I felt when I first stepped in the park grounds. I was with my cousins then, the feeling I felt was just pure bliss. It was incomparable, my heart was filled with happiness in an instant.

Through the years, Enchanted Kingdom remains to be the only world class theme park here in the Philippines, nothing even comes close. They have continuously improved in terms of rides and attractions that keeps people coming back for more.

The newest attraction i've seen the last time I went to Enchanted Kingdom was the 4D, which now I believe is the 7D. For those who haven't visited EK recently, sharing to you the 6 new attractions you should see when you visit Enchanted Kingdom again.

1. Space Shuttle

Fun and breathtaking-2 words to describe this ride that will keep you roller coaster ride that turns guests upside-down 6 times – 3 times forward and 3 times backward – all in two minutes!. I wouldn't pick this as the best ride, but yes! I would probably ride this again the next time I go back to EK!

2. Anchors Away

This is one of my favorite rides in Enchanted Kingdom. Usually when you are riding this kind of ride, you would only feel it when you seated at the very last row, but in Anchors away, you can feel your stomach turing in every seat. I recommend this ride as your first ride as long as you are riding in an empty stomach.

3. Ferris Wheel

This ride is romantic and fun. It is a great way to relax after a long day of riding different rides and going around the park. There is also a nice view at the top, making it a great way to end the day.
